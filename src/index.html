About me

Hello there!! I’m Namratha, or Nam for short. I’m a fullstack developer speciailizing in JavaScript and the NERDS stack (Node.js, Express, React, and Database using SQL).

I live in the music city of the US - Nashville, TN and I’m originally from a city called Mysore in Southern India which is referred to as the “City of Palaces”.

I have a background in Electronics and Communication/Electrical Engineering. Taking a combination of electrical and computer related courses during my masters got me interested in Software development and I took the big leap!

During quarantine, my past fields of study and my passion for coding motivated me to join the 13 week immersive Fullstack Academy coding bootcamp. My experience at FSA further solidified my passion for writing code and watching it come to life.

I look forward to further harnessing my technical and social skills to make a difference in real world problems with creative technology solutions.

When I’m not coding, you can find me enjoying good food and having an awesome time with my friends. I love to cook and enjoy good company :)

ByteDance Revolution

ByteDance Revolution is an interactive dance web application built using Node.js with Google Firebase, React and Posenet. The Posenet model was used from TensorFlow with React hooks on Audio and Webcam components to compare and grade players on pre-set dance routines, calculate and display scores on. The project workflow was managed using GitHub for branch control, merges and tasks Technologies used: JavaScript, Node.js, React, Firebase, TensorFlow, Posenet

Grace Shopper

A fully functional e-commerce site selling varieties of pasta, built in a span of 4 days and deployed on Heroku Worked closely with React components/Redux store for cart/checkout logic for a smooth user and guest experience. Also, integrated Google OAuth with Passport to enable logins using google accounts. Technologies used: React, React-Redux, Sequelize, Express.JS, PostgreSQL, Passport, GoogleOAuth.

Robotics Inc.

Robotics Inc. is a CRUD web app built over the course of one week while attending Fullstack Academy. This was a solo project assigned to students immediately after we had finished learning all the full stack technologies in the FSA curriculum. Tech used includes React, Redux, Express, Sequelize and PostgreSQL. This my first full stack web app built from start to finish. It served me by really helping to solidify all of the concepts, at every level of the stack, that I had learned in the prior six weeks or so.
